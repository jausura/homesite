Koulourakia (Greek Sesame Twist Cookies)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup + 6 Tablespoons Butter (room temp)
  • 1/4 Cup veggie oil
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons grated orange zest (aprox)
  • 1 Cup + 2 Tablespoons sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 Cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 Cup orange juice
  • Sesame seeds

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Beat together butter, oil, and orange zest
  3. Add in sugar, egg, and vanilla
  4. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder and baking soda. Add to creamed ingredients
  5. Ball and then roll little bits of dough then tie them into desired knots and place on cookie sheet.
  6. Coat cookies with sesame seeds
  7. Bake for 10 - 20 minutes (until brown on bottom and cooked through)

Bourbon Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Pecans
  • 8 Ounces Vanilla Wafers (enough to make 2 Cups)
  • 1/2 Cup unsweetened Dutch Cocoa, divided
  • 1 Cup Confectioners' Sugar, divided
  • 1/4 Cup Light Corn Syrup
  • 1/4 Cup Bourbon

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 325
  2. Bake Pecans on tray. Don't char. Should only take a couple minutes.
  3. Crumb Vanilla wafers.
  4. Chop Pecans.
  5. Mix wafers, pecans, 1/4 cup of cocoa, and 1/4 cup of confectioners sugar.
  6. Add corn syrup and bourbon.
  7. Sift remaining cocoa and sugar onto a plate.
  8. Roll balls in sugar/cocoa mixture
